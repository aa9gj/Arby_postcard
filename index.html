Arby (Abdullah) Abood

My career has been driven by a single principle: ensuring results are repeatable, defensible, and built on a foundation of integrity. For over five years as a computational biologist, I applied that principle to scientific discovery; today, I apply it to Quality Assurance.

In my former roles at Colgate-Palmolive and the University of Virginia, I built automated systems to manage and analyze complex biological data. The work demanded rigorous version control, adherence to established protocols, and a constant focus on creating structured, reviewable evidence from raw data. This experience in an SOP-driven R&D environment is the foundation of my compliance-focused mindset.

Now, as a QA professional and Six Sigma Yellow Belt (CSSGB in progress), I'm focused on what I care about most: audit readiness, data integrity (ALCOA+), and robust quality systems. I use my background in Biomedical Data Science to not only follow processes, but to help validate and improve them.

Outside of work, I’m into soccer, cooking, and translating complex ideas for non-specialists.
